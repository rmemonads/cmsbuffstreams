1. Introduction and General Policy

Buffstreams (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects the intellectual property rights of copyright owners and is committed to complying with applicable copyright laws, including the Digital Millennium Copyright Act of 1998 (DMCA) and relevant international regulations.

It is our policy to respond promptly to valid notices of alleged copyright infringement that comply with applicable legal requirements and are reported through the proper channels outlined below.

Important Clarification

Buffstreams operates strictly as an informational indexing and link aggregation platform.

  • We do not host, upload, stream, archive, store, or distribute any video files, live broadcasts, or copyrighted media on our own servers.

  • All media content referenced on buffstreamslive.cc is hosted on third-party websites and external servers that are publicly accessible on the internet.

  • We do not control, manage, or modify the content hosted by these third-party providers.

If you are a copyright owner and wish to request permanent removal of copyrighted material, we strongly recommend contacting the actual hosting provider directly. However, as a courtesy and in compliance with search engine and legal guidelines, Buffstreams will remove specific links from our index upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice.

4. Counter-Notification Procedure

If you believe that a link or reference was removed from Buffstreams due to error, misidentification, or lawful authorization, you may submit a counter-notification.

A valid counter-notice must include:

  • Your physical or electronic signature

  • Identification of the material that was removed and the location where it appeared before removal

  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the removal was due to mistake or misidentification

  • Your full name and contact information

Upon receipt of a valid counter-notice, we may restore the removed material unless the original complainant files legal action within the timeframe required by law.

5. Repeat Infringer Policy

Buffstreams maintains a policy of addressing repeat infringement in appropriate circumstances.

Because we do not host user-uploaded content, this policy primarily applies to:

  • Repeated submissions of infringing links

  • Abuse of reporting mechanisms

  • Intentional misrepresentation in notices or counter-notices

We reserve the right to restrict access, disable features, or take other appropriate action when necessary.
