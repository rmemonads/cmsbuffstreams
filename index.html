Adaptive Bitrate Streaming (ABR)

Buffstreams integrates Adaptive Bitrate Streaming (ABR). Adaptive Bitrate Streaming (ABR) is a technique for streaming multimedia. It detects bandwidth and CPU capacity, adjusting quality. If a connection drops, Buffstreams lowers bitrate to keep the video moving. Once stable, Adaptive Bitrate Streaming (ABR) scales resolution back to high definition.
